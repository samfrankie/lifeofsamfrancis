E-commerce Sales Forecasting

Developed a time-series forecasting model using Python and the Prophet library to predict future sales for an online retailer.

Project Overview

This project focuses on building a time-series forecasting model to predict future sales for an online retailer. The goal is to provide data-driven insights that can assist with inventory management, marketing strategy, and resource allocation. By accurately forecasting sales, the business can avoid stockouts and reduce excess inventory costs.

The project addresses the challenge of predicting future demand by developing a reliable forecasting model, which helps prevent lost sales, reduces inventory costs, and improves marketing efficiency.

Methodology

The analysis followed a standard data science workflow, from data collection and cleaning to model evaluation and visualization.

  • **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing**: Handled missing values, converted data types, and aggregated sales to a daily level.
  • **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**: Visualized sales trends over time to identify seasonality and the impact of holidays.
  • **Modeling**: A time-series forecasting model was developed using **Facebook's Prophet library**.
  • **Model Evaluation**: The model was evaluated using a holdout period to compare forecasted sales against actual sales, measuring performance with metrics like Mean Absolute Error (MAE) and Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE).
  • **Insights & Visualization**: The final model provides forecasts visualized on a graph, along with key insights on seasonal trends and special events.
